About the Conference

DMCMICS 2025 (De Gruyter Mathematics, Computing, and Management in Interdisciplinary Convergence Studies) is an international conference that brings together researchers, academics, and industry professionals from around the world to share their latest research findings and innovations in the fields of Mathematics, Computing, and Management.

The conference aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and convergence of ideas across these domains, promoting the exchange of knowledge and the development of innovative solutions to complex real-world problems.

Conference Highlights

  • International platform for research presentation
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration opportunities
  • Publication in reputed journals
  • Networking with global experts
  • Workshops and tutorials
  • Cultural exchange programs

Objectives

Knowledge Exchange

Facilitate the exchange of cutting-edge research and innovative ideas among participants from diverse backgrounds.

Collaboration

Promote interdisciplinary collaboration between mathematics, computing, and management disciplines.

Innovation

Encourage innovative solutions to complex problems through convergence of different fields of study.

Global Impact

Contribute to the advancement of knowledge with global impact and societal benefits.

About the Organizing Institute

Anjuman Institute of Technology and Management (AITM)

Anjuman Institute of Technology and Management (AITM) is a premier educational institution located in Bhatkal, Karnataka, India. Established with a vision to provide quality technical education, AITM has been at the forefront of academic excellence and innovation.

The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines including Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical Engineering, and Business Administration. AITM is committed to nurturing talent, fostering research, and preparing students for successful careers in the global marketplace.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a focus on holistic development, AITM continues to be a preferred destination for students seeking quality education in technology and management fields.
