Important Notes

Timing

All sessions will start and end on time. Please arrive 10 minutes early for each session.

Meals

Lunch and tea breaks are included in the registration fee. Dietary requirements should be mentioned during registration.

Presentations

Each technical presentation is allocated 15 minutes including Q&A. Presenters should report 30 minutes before their session.

Wi-Fi

Free high-speed Wi-Fi will be available throughout the campus for all registered participants.
