Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the conference?

You can register online through our registration page. Fill out the form with your details and complete the payment process.

What is the deadline for paper submission?

The deadline for full paper submission is April 15, 2025. Early submission is recommended.

Will the conference be held physically or virtually?

The conference will be held in hybrid mode - both physical attendance at the venue and virtual participation options will be available.

Do you provide accommodation facilities?

Yes, we offer accommodation arrangements for outstation participants. You can select this option during registration.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my registration?

Refund policies are mentioned in our terms and conditions. Please check the cancellation policy for details.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, certificates of participation will be provided to all registered attendees and presenters.
